• A six years journey.

  • I traveled a long way to be here. I left 6 years ago looking to join local music scenes. That's always been my only true compass.

    Cluj, Romania -> Eastbourne -> Brighton -> Bristol -> London.

  • I never had a developer job.

  • My dream is to become a UI/UX designer and work alongside people on things that have meaning and share a common purpose. For it I need to learn how to work and move within a colective.

    I have been working in hospitality for a while. I started out as a Kitchen Porter in 2018, moving up to waiter as I got more confident with the language. I stuck to the bartender role since 2021, working all around London. I like getting out of the house and meeting people. I'm fascinated with London and how much it can teach you if you are willing to listen.

    I can’t do my UI/UX part without knowing how to code. I wouldn’t be able to leave my teammates struggle with a technical problem while I sit around and watch. I’d need to jump in.
